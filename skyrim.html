Skyrim

Skyrim was probably one of the first story-oriented games I played. As an avid reader, I loved jumping into a story world where I could actually see unique character designs and how magical powers might look. I also loved the skill system. It was my first experience with leveling a character so much that your character was actually unstoppable.

Oh, and don't even get me started on the character selection screen. I spent hours trying to pick what I wanted my character to look like, but more importantly, the different focus options. I think this was also the first game that had less limited presets for different body types (and surprise, female characters!)

Skyrim's intro was great, and the memes that came from it were even better. If you know, you know.
