Apex Legends

Ah yes, the good ol' battle royal. Also known as a huge money pit. Unfortunately for me, I will admit to spending some real life money in this game. Too much. Anyway, this was the first battle royal that had me hooked. The game combines gun skill with unique character powers/abilities that made it super fun. Also, the concept art is absolutely stunning! (see below).

The different skins that you can get in this game are absolutely phenomenal too. Don't get me wrong, the base skins are great (as shown in the left columns in the grid below), but their specialty skins are incredibly creative and cool.
