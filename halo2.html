Halo 2

Halo 2 was the first video game I really remember playing as a kid. My brothers and I would fight over the controller that had the least number of issues connecting to the xbox. Whoever lost usually ended up with the controller that had a faulty cord that would only work if you held it at exactly the right angle.


Some reasons Halo 2 is still one of my favorites

  • I grew up playing the game with my dad and 5 brothers.
  • I have fond memories of referring to the alien blood as "purple jelly" at every possible chance.
  • It was one of few reasons my parents let me stay up late as a kid.
  • My dad usually shared his stash of oreos with us during our Halo marathons.
  • The energy sword is still one of the coolest weapon designs in any game.
