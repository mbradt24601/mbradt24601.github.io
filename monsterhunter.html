Monster Hunter

This game says exactly what it is in the title. You do, in fact, hunt monsters (crazy, I know). It's kind of like the reason all the movies need Godzilla dead. When he gets too big and powerful, he destroys everything. These monsters do the same thing in this game but on a godlike level, so somebody's got to take them out. Also, the armor you can earn in this game from defeating these monsters is amazing. (Can you tell I like cosmetics?)

I will admit that this is one of those games that I have loved, but haven't sent as much time on as I'd like to. My husband plays this game a lot, so I have spent more time watching him play it than I've played myself, but only because I'm being responsible and doing my homework during that time.
